Viking Revenge (2017)

For this game, I challenged myself to make a game in one month. As I was also a student, the time spent on this project was very limited. This was my first solo project, so I did everything myself. The game was very much a joke, based on the long-term joke that Danes hate Swedes.

Since I am not an artist by trade, I decided to go with pixel art. While it is not the most beautiful (by far), to me it was a lot easier to make something mediocre and recognizable than with a higher resolution.
